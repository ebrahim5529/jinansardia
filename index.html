Company Overview

JinanSardia International Supply Chain Management Co., Ltd. is a specialized supply chain solutions provider dedicated to the sourcing, coordination, and delivery of medical consumables, medical furniture, and trucks and trailers. Our comprehensive approach supports clients by streamlining procurement processes, managing supplier relationships, coordinating logistics operations, and ensuring seamless cross-border fulfillment.

We are committed to ensuring reliable supply continuity and cost-effective operations for our clients across healthcare and transportation sectors. Through our structured approach to quality control and efficient logistics coordination, we deliver solutions that meet the most demanding requirements while maintaining the highest standards of service excellence.

Our History

Founded to address the growing need for dependable, end-to-end supply chain services across healthcare and transportation sectors, JinanSardia International has developed a focused operating model built around vetted suppliers, structured quality control, and efficient logistics coordination.

Over time, our company has expanded its category coverage and strengthened its supply network to serve diverse customer requirements. We have continuously evolved our processes and capabilities to meet the changing demands of global markets while maintaining our commitment to excellence and reliability.

Our Mission

To deliver dependable, compliant, and efficient supply chain solutions that help customers secure essential medical and transportation products with confidence, speed, and consistency.

Core Business Areas

We specialize in three critical sectors, providing comprehensive supply chain solutions tailored to meet specific industry requirements and operational demands.

Medical Consumables Supply Chain

  • Sourcing and supply coordination for disposable and essential medical consumables
  • Supplier qualification, order consolidation, and delivery planning
  • Support for consistent availability and responsive replenishment

Medical Furniture Supply Chain

  • End-to-end sourcing and fulfillment of medical furniture for healthcare settings
  • Product coordination for clinics, hospitals, and project-based needs
  • Packaging, shipping, and delivery management aligned with customer schedules

Trucks and Trailer Supply Chain

  • Sourcing and supply coordination for trucks, trailers, and related equipment
  • Supplier management and delivery scheduling to meet operational demands
  • Logistics support for fleet procurement and deployment
